Co-sharer Affidavit

(For Joint Ownership of Property)

As per HP Home Stay Rules 2025 - ANNEXURE-II

When is this required?
This affidavit is required when the property is jointly owned by multiple persons (as per revenue records - Jamabandi) and one person is applying for Home Stay registration on behalf of all co-sharers.

Important Notes:

  • This affidavit must be executed on non-judicial stamp paper of appropriate value.
  • All co-sharers as per Jamabandi must sign this affidavit.
  • The affidavit should be notarized or attested by a First Class Magistrate.
  • Attach a copy of the Jamabandi/Tatima showing all co-sharers.
  • Alternative: A Patwari Report confirming ownership can be submitted instead of this affidavit.
